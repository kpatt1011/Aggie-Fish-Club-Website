Throughout the year, 19 upperclassmen are dedicated to making this the greatest year of your life!! You will find that you will grow and learn things about yourself that you never could have imagined. We know because we've been there before. All of these staff members were once freshman too, and we remember what it was like to be in your shoes. Why have we devoted our year and at least half of our college career to this organization? To help you make the transition throughout the year, and along the way find some great skills and true friends! So meet the staff of AFC 12X13!!!
