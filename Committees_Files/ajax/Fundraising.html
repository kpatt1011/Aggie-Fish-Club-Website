Fundraising


In this awesome committee, freshmen work alongside each other on a quest to gain sponsorship and collect donations from local businesses to benefit our great philanthropies, Phoebe's Home and New Horizons. Phoebe's Home is a 24-hour emergency shelter for victims of domestic violence, and New Horizons is a home for abused and neglected children. AFC is proud to say that every single dollar we raise at a Fundraising event goes towards these amazing organizations. Events such as profit shares, post-game Reed Arena clean-ups and bake sales are led and organized by Fundraising. Most importantly the Annual AFC Gold Tournament is completely run by the Fundraising freshman and is always a blast. GET MONEY!!!
