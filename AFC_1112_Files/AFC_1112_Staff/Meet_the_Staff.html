Throughout the year, 19 upperclassmen are dedicated to making this the greatest year of your life!! You will find that you will grow and learn things about yourself that you never could have imagined. We know because we've been there before. To help you make the transition throughout the year, and along the way find some great skills and true friends! So meet the staff of AFC 2011.2012!!!
